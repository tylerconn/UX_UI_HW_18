past projects

Department of Energy Website Redesign

The goal of this project was to redesign the Department of Energy website in order to improve its usability. My focus for this redesign was on reorganizing the information arch-itecture because a confusing navigation was consistently brought up in my research with users. Following this redesign, users indicated that the navigation was more intuitive and, as a result, they were more likely to use the site.
